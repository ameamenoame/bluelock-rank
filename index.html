Dota 2 Leaderboard Counter

How do I get my player ID number?

You can try searching for your profile on sites such as Dotabuff, OpenDota, Stratz. Your player ID will be the 8 or 9 digit number that's in your profile URL on these sites.

For example: https://stratz.com/players/126212866 where the player ID is 126212866

Does this work for non-Immortal players?

This only works for Immortal players with a leaderboard position in their respective region.
